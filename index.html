Rylith Academy's curriculum empowers schools to offer a progressive, skill-based learning path for their students. By adding courses that focus on communication, leadership, entrepreneurship, and digital literacy, you provide your students with the essential skills they need for both academic and personal success.

The NEP 2020 advocates for holistic, interdisciplinary learning and prepares students for real-world challenges. Rylith Academy's courses are specifically designed to support the implementation of NEP's vision. Our curriculum encourages experiential learning, critical thinking, and the development of emotional and social intelligence, ensuring that students are not only knowledgeable but also well-prepared to face the future.

Our content is designed to complement traditional academic learning, ensuring that students build a strong foundation in subjects that matter in the modern world. Whether it's teaching financial literacy or fostering entrepreneurial thinking, our courses provide practical skills that students can apply immediately in their lives. This enhances the school's educational offerings, providing tangible value to both students and parents.

Rylith Academy is built to integrate with your existing curriculum and teaching methods, making it easy for school management to adopt and implement. We offer ongoing support, training for educators, and a full suite of resources to ensure a smooth integration process.

Our focus is on creating tangible results. Students who complete our programs are equipped with the skills to make informed decisions, communicate effectively, lead with confidence, and innovate in their chosen fields. Schools can proudly showcase their commitment to developing the leaders of tomorrow by offering Rylith Academy's forward-thinking curriculum.

Rylith Academy: Empowering the Future of Education

Rylith Academy is an innovative educational initiative designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in the 21st century. Our programs focus on holistic development, blending academic excellence with critical life skills. We partner with schools to provide progressive, real-world learning experiences that align with the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020.

Aligned with NEP 2020

In line with the vision of NEP 2020, Rylith Academy's curriculum supports the development of well-rounded, adaptable, and critical-thinking students. The NEP encourages a shift toward skill-based education, and our programs are designed to integrate seamlessly with this framework. By partnering with Rylith Academy, schools can enhance their existing curriculum and ensure they are fostering not only academic growth but also personal development in their students.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to provide schools with a comprehensive, skills-based curriculum that goes beyond traditional academic learning. We empower students to become problem-solvers, leaders, and responsible global citizens by focusing on core life skills that makes them thrive.

We envision a future where students are not just academically prepared, but also equipped with the emotional intelligence, creativity, and practical skills needed to thrive in an ever-evolving world. Our aim is to become a trusted partner for schools in creating impactful educational experiences that truly add value to their students' lives.

The Founding Team

At Rylith, we are a group of passion-driven, new-age edupreneurs committed to reshaping education for the modern world. Our team is made up of forward-thinking individuals who blend innovation with education to create meaningful, real-world learning experiences that prepare students for success in every aspect of their lives.
